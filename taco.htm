The Original Way to Bueno

This Bueno classic is how we got our name! A crunchy corn tortilla cradles your choice of lightly seasoned ground beef, all white meat chicken or fajita steak, along with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes and freshly grated blend of cheddar and pepper jack cheese for a taste that’s nothing but Bueno.
